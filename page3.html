Background of the artist Popular Songs
Alvin James P. Maluntac (born on August 19, 1991). Who is known as AL James is a Filipino Rapper and a Song writer.

AL James graduated in his degree in fine arts and design from University of Santo Thomas. Then afterward he is now intricated in hip-hop where he met his collaborators. Which is collectively known as "Baryo Berde Atbp", and there he has a group of 11 members in the collaboration.

Since the year 2017, his song which his success of writing "Ngayong Gabi" has collected with over 25 million viewers in just 18 months. Its lyric video has racked up almost about 60 million views as of his writing and the official music video which is viewed over about more than 6.6 million
